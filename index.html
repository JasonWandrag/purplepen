It's also why we recommend working closely with you throughout your editing journey, and investing our passion into your project!

About Me

My name is Kerrin and I'm the owner at Purple Pen Proofreading and Copy Editing.

My love of reading and of good books, led me to explore a career in copy editing, which to me feels like pure pleasure instead of work!

I studied at the College of Media and Publishing, based in London.

I specialize in perfecting translated text into British English, American English and South African English, and also correcting and improving the English between those languages.

What I most enjoy working on are Christian fiction and non-fiction works, and feel that my contribution could aid your ministry in a small way.

I also love self-help text, coaching guides and any writing with a positive and uplifting tone - it teaches me too as I work on it!

What We Do

Proofreading

We find and fix spelling, grammar and punctuation mistakes, so your work can be presented as a professional and error-free piece of writing.

Copy Editing

We edit at a sentence level, line by line, to improve sentence structure and enhance readability, while keeping your unique voice intact.

Style & Consistency Checks

We follow the style guide specified by you, or we create one, to be used throughout your project to ensure consistency.
